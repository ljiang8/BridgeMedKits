About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and story.

Our Mission

BridgeMedKits is a student-led nonprofit focused on delivering custom-built medical kits to underserved clinics around the world. By providing essential health supplies and resources, we aim to support healthcare workers and make a global impact on healthcare accessibility.

Long-Term Vision

Our vision is to build a more equitable healthcare system by ensuring that no clinic is held back by a lack of basic supplies. We believe that students can play a real role in global health by listening to local partners, designing thoughtful solutions, and staying accountable to the communities we serve.

Our Story

A timeline of how BridgeMedKits has grown.

2023

BridgeMedKits Founded

Started as a student project after witnessing firsthand the supply gaps facing clinics in underserved communities.

2024

First International Shipment

Shipped our first set of custom-built medical kits to clinics in Africa, working closely with local partners.

2025

Expanding Kit Types

Began developing specialized maternal and pediatric kits to better match the needs of providers and patients.

Future

Growing Our Impact

With new partners and supporters, we aim to scale shipments, deepen relationships with clinics, and empower more students to lead.
