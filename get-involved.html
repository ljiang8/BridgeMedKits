Partnership Opportunities

We're always looking to collaborate with clinics, nonprofits, student groups, and local businesses. If you're interested in partnering with BridgeMedKits, we'd love to hear from you.

Partnership opportunities include co-designing kits, sponsoring shipments, hosting supply drives, or launching a school-based chapter that helps fund and assemble kits.
