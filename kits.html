What Our Kits Look Like

Photos and descriptions of the kits we ship to partner clinics.

Clinic Kit Photo

Standard Clinic Kit

A mix of general-purpose supplies such as gloves, gauze, dressings, bandages, basic instruments, and other essentials for day-to-day patient care.

Maternal Kit Photo

Maternal Health Kit

Designed to support prenatal, delivery, and postpartum care. Includes items like sterile pads, clean delivery supplies, and postpartum essentials. (In development.)

Pediatric Kit Photo

Pediatric Kit

Focused on caring for children, with appropriately sized supplies and child-focused care items. (In development.)
